Turn-Based Battles in TotalBattle

Squads Rule the Fight

In TotalBattle, combat is a strategic showdown where preparation meets opportunity. You and your enemy assemble armies from different troop types—each with unique strengths, like brutal attackers, sturdy defenders, or crafty specialists. Once the battle kicks off, an algorithm locks in the order your squads will strike, and the real test begins.

How It Works

Every cycle, each squad gets exactly one chance to hit. The order is set, the first strike is a 50-50 coin toss, and then it’s a relentless back-and-forth. The twist? The side with more squads lands more blows per cycle. Bring five squads against your enemy’s three, and you’re hitting twice more every round. It’s a numbers game with a tactical edge—more squads don’t just mean more power, they mean more opportunities to wear down your foe.

Stack Calculator: Your Tactical Edge

Want to maximize your odds? The Stack Calculator’s got your back. This handy tool lets you calculate with precision the exact type and size of squads you need for an optimal battle, ensuring your army’s perfectly tuned to dominate. The fight rolls on, cycle after cycle, until one side’s squads are wiped out. Stack your army smartly, balance your troop types, and with the Stack Calculator’s help, you’ll outlast and outpunch anyone who steps into the fray.
